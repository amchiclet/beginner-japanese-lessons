Beginner Japanese

Tourist Japanese

A short, practical course for speaking and reading enough Japanese to travel: order, shop, find your way, and read the signs. Built around three core sentence patterns and high-frequency words, with audio, flashcard drills, and a bit of history sprinkled in.

How to use this. Do one lesson at a time. Each is short and gives one usable win. Do the flashcard drill, then come back and restart it a day later — spacing is what makes it stick. Tap 🔊 to hear every word. See how to study.
