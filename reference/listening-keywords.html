Listening keywords

Staff speak fast, polite Japanese (keigo), so their sentences are long. You don't parse the sentence — you catch one keyword and answer short. This page collects the keywords worth listening for, by scenario. It grows as we cover more.

The two answers that cover most yes/no questions: お願いします (onegaishimasu) = "yes, please" · 大丈夫です (daijōbu desu) = "no, I'm fine."
